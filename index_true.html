About Me

I’m a second year undergraduate student completing a Physics honours degree with a major in Mathematics at Bishop’s University. I am the Senior Editor for the Natural Sciences and Mathematics for the Bishop’s Undergraduate Research Journal, and a co-lead for the Astronomy, Mathematics & Physics Society. I am also serving as the Chair of the Inclusivity & Diversity Committee at the Royal Astronomical Society of Canada. In my free time, I like to read, write, practice yoga and knit.

Research

I am currently researching the analogy between accretion in stellar mass black holes in x-ray binary systems and accretion in supermassive black holes found in quasars using data from the Chandra X-ray Observatory and the Hubble Space Telescope.
